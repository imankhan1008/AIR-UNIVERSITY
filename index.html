Air University

A Societal Mission


Air University was founded nearly 150 years ago on a foundation of societal purpose. Our mission is to contribute to the world by educating students for lives of leadership and meaningful contribution with integrity; advancing essential knowledge and fostering creativity; leading pioneering research for effective solutions; and accelerating innovative ideas to amplify their impact globally.
